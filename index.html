Introduction

This Nepal Bhasa Sahitya Sammelan (Also known as Tamunjya) is a special program organized for the preservation, promotion, and exchanging ideas on Nepali language literature, language, and culture. Various writers, scholars, and cultural experts will participate in it.

Program Schedule

October 18

Time Program
10:00 AM Opening ceremony
11:00 AM Group Poetry Reading - Writers A,B,C,D,E
1:00 PM Essay Reading - Literary D,E
3:00 PM Discussion and interaction

October 19

Time Program
10:00 AM Group Poetry Reading - Writers F, G
11:00 AM Memoirs and Story Readings - Writers H, I
1:00 PM Essay Reading - Literary J, K
3:00 PM Closing Program
